Honey Garlic Chicken

Honey Garlic Chicken is a savory and slightly sweet dish made with tender chicken cooked in a rich honey and garlic sauce.

szechuan chicken

Szechuan Chicken is a bold and spicy dish featuring chicken cooked in a tangy, savory sauce made with Szechuan peppercorns, soy sauce, and garlic. Its fiery heat and aromatic flavors make it a favorite in Chinese cuisine.

Tea Eggs

Tea Eggs are a popular Chinese snack made by boiling eggs and then simmering them in a mixture of tea, soy sauce, and spices. The result is a flavorful, marbled egg with a savory, aromatic taste.
